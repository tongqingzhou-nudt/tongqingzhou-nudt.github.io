Tongqing Zhou

Tongqing Zhou received his bachelor's, master's, and Ph.D. degree from College of Computer, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), in 2012, 2015, and 2018, respectively. His doctoral dissertation was awarded the Outstanding Dissertation Award of Hunan Province. He is currently an Assistant Researcher in the College of Computer at NUDT. His research interests include mobile crowdsensing, data privacy, and AI security. He was a research associate at Department of Computing in Hong Kong Polytechnic University (2017), in the group of Prof. Bin Xiao and was a PostDoc in the group of Profs. Jinshu Su and Zhiping Cai at NUDT (2019-2022).
